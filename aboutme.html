About Me

My name is Sama Yacout. I am a 14-year old student at Colonel By SS.
I am from Alexandria, Egypt, and born in Kuwait. At the age of 3, I moved to Canada,
and learned English and French. I enjoy many foods, including poutine, shawarma,
burger, and many more! One of many places I enjoyed visiting was Toronto. During
the summer of 2017, my family and I went to Toronto for 3 days. During those days,
we went to the CN Tower, Canada's Wonderland, the aquariam, and many more. I have
many hobbies, including drawing, coding, reading, and more! When I am older, I
aspire to become a software engineer. Specifically, a front-end web developper.
Since the age of 12, I have always been interested by how websites and apps are
formed. I have made it my goal to answer these questions. Besides coding, I really
enjoy drawing! Specifically, I enjoy drawing realistic objects with colored pencils.
I hope to further advance my drawing abilities. One of the most relaxing activities
that I enjoy is reading. It is very calming and helps me relax my brain.
